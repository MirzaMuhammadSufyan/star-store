50 Best Free AI Websites Everyone Should Bookmark in 2026

Writing, coding, images, video, study help, and productivity — fifty genuinely useful free AI tools, organised by what you need to do, not hype.

Fifty bookmarks sounds excessive until you realise most people rediscover the same three tools every week. This list focuses on free tiers that remain useful — not trials that expire in forty-eight hours.

Organised by job-to-be-done. Pin the sections that match your week.

Writing and Editing

1. ChatGPT (free tier) — drafts, outlines, rewrites 2. Claude (free tier) — long-form tone and document uploads 3. Google Gemini — research tied to Google apps 4. Grammarly — grammar and clarity checks 5. QuillBot — paraphrasing and summarising 6. Hemingway Editor — readability scoring (web app) 7. LanguageTool — multilingual grammar support 8. Notion AI (limited free) — inside notes and wikis

Coding and Development

9. GitHub Copilot (free for verified students/OSS) — inline code suggestions 10. Codeium — free autocomplete for many IDEs 11. Replit AI — build and explain small projects in browser 12. Phind — developer-focused search answers 13. Tabnine (free tier) — local-friendly completions 14. AskCodi — snippets and docstrings 15. SQLChat — natural language to SQL practice

Leonardo.ai (daily tokens) — stylised images 20. Ideogram — text-in-image strength 21. Remove.bg — background removal 22. Cleanup.pictures — object removal 23. Upscale.media — resolution enhancement

Tome (limited) — slide narratives from prompts 42. Gamma — deck and doc generation 43. Fireflies.ai (free tier) — call notes 44. Mem — AI-tagged notes

Marketing and SEO (Free Tiers)

45. AnswerThePublic (limited) — question keywords 46. Ubersuggest (free searches) — keyword ideas 47. AlsoAsked — People Also Ask trees 48. Hugging Face Spaces — open demos for countless models 49. Google Trends — demand timing 50. Excalidraw + AI plugins — whiteboard diagrams fast

How to Use This List Without Drowning

Pick five tools maximum for your role. Install nothing else for thirty days.

| Role | Start with | |------|------------| | Student | Gemini or ChatGPT, Perplexity, Quizlet, Otter | | Developer | Codeium, Phind, GitHub docs, Remove.bg for assets | | Creator | CapCut, Canva, Claude for scripts, Ideogram | | Freelancer | Notion AI, Grammarly, Otter, Zapier |

Free Tier Caveats

  • Usage caps reset daily or monthly — plan around limits
  • Free models may lag behind paid versions
  • Uploading confidential files to consumer tools is risky
  • Outputs need human verification, especially facts and stats
Bookmark this page, then delete bookmarks you never open. The best AI stack is the smallest one that clears your actual backlog — not the longest list on the internet.
